Unit 10: Advances in Natural Language Processing

Unit 10 focused on the rapid advancements in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and how modern machine learning techniques are transforming the way machines understand human language. In this unit, I explored the evolution of NLP from traditional rule-based approaches to advanced deep learning models. Special attention was given to Transformer-based architectures such as BERT, GPT, and T5, which have significantly improved tasks like text generation, translation, and summarisation. I also learned about the role of self-supervised learning in enhancing model performance and scalability. Additionally, the unit covered important evaluation metrics and introduced the concept of MLOps in NLP, including model monitoring and continuous improvement.

Key Learnings:

  • Understanding the evolution of NLP from rule-based systems to deep learning models.
  • Exploring Transformer-based architectures like BERT, GPT, and T5 and their applications.
  • Learning the impact of self-supervised learning on NLP advancements.
  • Understanding evaluation metrics such as BLEU, ROUGE, and perplexity.
  • Gaining insight into the role of MLOps in deploying, monitoring, and retraining NLP models.
  • Developing the ability to apply, evaluate, and improve NLP models using modern techniques.
