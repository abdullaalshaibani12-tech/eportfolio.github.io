Weekly Activities


Unit 1 : Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

In Unit 1, the focus was on gaining a foundational understanding of Artificial Intelligence and its significance in modern society. I learned about the origins and evolution of AI, exploring how it relates to human intelligence and its expanding role across industries. The unit emphasized the importance of ethical and governance considerations in AI development and the professionalism required to work responsibly in the field. Additionally, I gained awareness of the wide range of AI applications and their impact on organizations and communities.

Reflection
I developed a clear understanding of Artificial Intelligence, its history, and its connection to human intelligence. Learning about AI’s origins and evolution deepened my appreciation for how the technology has progressed and influenced modern society. The exploration of diverse applications highlighted AI’s broad impact on industries and communities. Most importantly, the discussion on ethical, safety, and governance issues emphasized the need for responsible development and professional integrity. This unit encouraged me to view AI not just as a technological tool but as a powerful force requiring careful consideration of its social, legal, and ethical implications
