Team Work Assessment

As part of the teamwork assessment, Deane, Jordan, and I collaborated to complete a project where we were required to choose between two topics: a) Digital Forensics or b) Academic Research Online. Our group selected Academic Research Online and worked together to research, draft, and submit a report. We coordinated our efforts through online meetings, recorded discussions, and collaborative platforms such as Google Drive, Discord, and WhatsApp to ensure smooth communication and task management.


My Contribution:
I was responsible for Phase 3 of the project, working alongside Deane to complete the required research and documentation. This included ensuring access to shared resources, collaborating on content, and contributing to the development of the final report.


Key Outcomes of the Teamwork:
  • Effective use of online collaboration platforms (Google Drive, Discord, WhatsApp)
  • Clear division of tasks: Jordan on report structure, Deane on diagrams, and myself on Phase 3
  • Successful coordination and communication to overcome challenges such as document access and workload distribution
