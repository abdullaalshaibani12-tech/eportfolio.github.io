Weekly Activities

Unit 7: Introduction to Artificial Neural Networks

Unit 7 introduced the fundamental concepts of Artificial Neural Networks (ANN), a powerful machine learning approach inspired by the human brain. In this unit, I explored how ANN models mimic biological neurons to learn from data and improve decision-making over time. I gained insight into how these networks are structured and how they adapt based on input data. The unit also highlighted the growing importance of ANN in modern applications such as automation, marketing, and intelligent systems, especially in the context of Industry 4.0. Additionally, I studied the key mathematical functions and algorithms that drive neural networks and enable them to perform complex tasks.

Key Learnings:

  • Understanding the analogy between biological neurons and artificial neural networks.
  • Gaining a detailed understanding of the core algorithms behind ANN.
  • Exploring different activation functions used in neural networks.
  • Learning how ANN models are designed and developed for simple tasks.
  • Developing the ability to critically analyze and interpret ANN-based systems.
  • Understanding the role of ANN in real-world applications and emerging technologies.
