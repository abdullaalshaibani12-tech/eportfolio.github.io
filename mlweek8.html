Unit 8: Training an Artificial Neural Network

Unit 8 focused on the training process of Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) and how these models improve their performance over time. Building on the previous unit, I learned how neural networks identify errors in their predictions and use those errors to adjust their internal parameters. A key concept explored was backpropagation, which enables the network to update connection weights efficiently and minimize error. This unit also emphasized the importance of training in making ANN models accurate and reliable. Additionally, I explored real-world applications of ANN and how trained models are used to solve practical problems across various domains.

Key Learnings:

  • Understanding how neural networks learn from errors during training.
  • Learning the concept and working of backpropagation in ANN.
  • Exploring how weights are adjusted to improve model accuracy.
  • Gaining experience in designing and developing more advanced ANN models.
  • Understanding real-life applications of trained neural networks.
  • Developing the ability to critically evaluate and interpret ANN research and models.
