Weekly Activities


Unit 1: Introduction to R and Descriptive Statistics

Unit 1 focused on understanding the fundamentals of R programming and its application in business statistics. I learned about different interfaces and packages used in R, along with how to import, manipulate, and save data efficiently. The unit also explored the importance of statistics in business decision-making, emphasizing how data-driven insights can improve performance and strategic planning. Additionally, I examined how data can be summarized in various formats and developed an understanding of different types of measurement scales. Finally, I practiced determining levels of measurement and performing descriptive statistical analyses using R to summarize and interpret datasets.

Key Learnings:

  • Understanding different interfaces and packages available in R.
  • Importing, managing, and saving data effectively in R.
  • Recognizing the importance of statistics in business applications.
  • Exploring methods to summarize and represent data in different formats.
  • Understanding measurement scales and their relevance to data analysis.
  • Performing descriptive statistical analysis in R based on measurement levels.
