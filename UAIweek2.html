Unit 2: Artificial Intelligence – The Benefits and Threats to Business

In Unit 2, the focus was on exploring how Artificial Intelligence can be applied within businesses and the opportunities and risks that come with it. I learned to evaluate the practical benefits of AI technologies, such as improved efficiency, data-driven decision-making, and enhanced customer experiences. The unit also highlighted the challenges organizations face, including implementation costs, data privacy concerns, and workforce impacts.

Reflection
This unit expanded my understanding of AI beyond theory, showing how it transforms business operations while introducing complex ethical and governance considerations. I gained insight into balancing AI’s potential for innovation with the need for transparency, fairness, and compliance with regulations. Recognizing the importance of advising companies on responsible AI practices strengthened my appreciation of the AI professional’s role as both a technical expert and an ethical guide
