Weekly Activities


Unit 1: Introduction to Machine Learning

Unit 1 focused on understanding the fundamentals of Machine Learning (ML) and its role in modern technology and business applications. I learned about the evolution of ML, from its origins to current trends, and explored how algorithms can be applied to solve real-world problems. The unit highlighted the interplay between big data, ML, and artificial intelligence (AI), emphasizing how real-time data enables machines to make accurate, autonomous decisions. Additionally, I examined the challenges and opportunities associated with ML, including ethical considerations and common pitfalls, while identifying the skills required to become proficient in this field.

Key Learnings:

  • Understanding the history, current state, and future potential of machine learning.
  • Recognizing the role of big data in enabling machine learning algorithms.
  • Exploring the relationship between machine learning, big data analytics, and artificial intelligence.
  • Identifying challenges, limitations, and ethical considerations in using algorithms.
  • Learning about the skills and knowledge required to excel in machine learning.
  • Understanding common pitfalls in ML applications and strategies to address them.
