Unit 3: Reading and Managing Data in R

Unit 3 covered the simple process of reading data into RStudio and managing it effectively for analysis. I learned how to import different types of data files and explore their structure once they are loaded into R. The unit focused on examining both entire datasets and specific variables to gain insights into data composition and quality. Additionally, I explored how to convert variable types to ensure proper data handling and consistency during analysis. Finally, the unit addressed managing missing values in R, an essential step in preparing clean and reliable datasets for further statistical work.

Key Learnings:

  • Reading in different types of data files using RStudio.
  • Examining entire datasets to understand data structure and content.
  • Exploring and analyzing specific variables within a dataset.
  • Converting variable types for consistency and accurate analysis.
  • Managing and handling missing values effectively in R.
